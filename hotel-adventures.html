Adventures

Treehotel is located in the tall pine forest with spectacular views of the Lule River. The tree rooms are suspended 4-10 metres above the ground and are accessible by ramp, bridge, or electric stairs. Upon checking in at The Guesthouse, it’s a five-minute walk to Treehotel. Breakfast, lunch and dinner is served in our Treehotel Guesthouse restaurant.

Enjoy delightful breakfasts, leisurely lunches, and memorable dinners in a setting that harmonizes with the natural wonders that surround you. Your Treehotel stay is a gateway to unforgettable adventures in the Lapland wilderness.

Husky tours and adventure

Experience the thrill of dog sledding through the snowy wilderness on a husky adventure! This exciting activity is perfect for families, couples, and groups of friends. You'll get to meet the friendly huskies, learn about their history and training, and then take them for a ride through the snow.

Our husky adventures are led by experienced guides who will make sure you have a safe and enjoyable experience. We offer a variety of different tours to choose from, so you can find the perfect one for your group. So what are you waiting for? Book your husky adventure today and experience the magic of the winter wilderness!

Hike till dawn

In a beautiful area with steep cliffs, lichen-filled trees and tranquil lakes waits a beautiful hike up and down forest hills. The climb up will be rewarded with a mile-long view in no mans land. There is a chance to see wildlife such as moose and reindeer both on the hike and on your way to get there. Your guide will tell you about the nature and the different species that are unique to this area. Tour booked by Treehotel

The stately Storforsen

Come with us to the stately Storforsen, one of the highest unrestricted white-water rapids in Europe. Let yourself be carried away by a hike next to the thundering river and also enjoy the quieter brooks running out between the rocks. There are footbridges in the area allowing everyone to make their way out to the rapids. There is also a forestry and log driving museum here. During the visit, our guide will cook you lunch over an open fire and on hot days you can go for a dip in one of the natural pools formed amongst the rocks.
