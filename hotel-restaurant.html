Restaurants

The restaurant is a significant part of being at one with our spectacular Swedish nature. We offer all kinds of local Swedish delicacies from the northern pantry, whether it’s wild game or our finest hand-picked berries. Taste the highest quality food of the current season and enjoy the creative simplicity of local produce. Our chefs will tell you exactly what’s on the table and offer a personalized experience tailored to your dining needs.

We now have the highest quality catering to match the guest service. The chefs ability to create a variety of unique dishes from the local produce made it the perfect fit for us.

Riverside Grill - Al Fresco Dining by the Water:

At the Treehotel's Riverside Grill, we invite you to relish the simplicity of life by the river. Our outdoor dining experience lets you bask in the natural beauty of the nearby river while savoring grilled specialties and barbecue favorites. Our menu showcases a diverse range of meats, seafood, and vegetarian options, all prepared with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. Relax at our picnic-style tables with colorful umbrellas, and let the soothing sounds of the flowing river serenade you. Combine your meal with riverside activities like fishing, swimming, or simply lounging by the riverbank.

As you dine at the Riverside Grill, you'll find that our commitment to sustainability extends beyond just our ingredients. Our eco-friendly practices, such as using reusable tableware and minimizing waste, are in harmony with the pristine natural surroundings.

The open-air ambiance and the gentle breeze off the river make this a perfect spot for families, couples, and nature enthusiasts alike.

Forest Bistro - A Treetop Culinary Retreat:

Nestled high among the treetops, the Forest Bistro at Treehotel offers an unparalleled dining experience for those seeking a taste of nature's elegance. Our gourmet menu, inspired by the pristine Swedish wilderness, features seasonal delicacies like reindeer, wild mushrooms, and Arctic char. Candlelit tables suspended among the trees create an intimate ambiance, providing panoramic views of the surrounding forest. With the soft sounds of nature as your background music, savor every bite of your meal in this enchanting forest canopy retreat. Reserve your table in advance for an unforgettable culinary journey.

At the Forest Bistro, we believe that dining should be a sensory journey, where every dish tells a story of the land it comes from.

Northern Lights Lounge - Aurora Borealis Viewing Restaurant:

Prepare to be awestruck as you dine under the dazzling Northern Lights at the Treehotel's Northern Lights Lounge. Our unique dining experience offers panoramic views of the night sky, perfect for witnessing the mystical Aurora Borealis. Our menu features Nordic-inspired dishes using local ingredients, allowing you to savor your meal while keeping a watchful eye on the night's celestial performance. Within our cozy, glass-enclosed dining space, you'll have the chance to witness the breathtaking Northern Lights as you indulge in a culinary adventure. Reservations are highly recommended during the Northern Lights season to ensure a front-row seat to this natural wonder.

Our dedicated team at the Northern Lights Lounge takes pride in curating an unforgettable dining experience that aligns with the celestial magic of the Northern Lights. To complement the awe-inspiring show above, our attentive staff is well-versed in the nuances of both the menu and the night sky.
